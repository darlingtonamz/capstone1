To the givers.

Site lists the top gifts in a home page
Users can browse through gifts

Your employees, friends, and family are your ambassadors. Make them glow with love.

To the receiver.

User receives alert via Push-notification or online notification, or SMS. Gift is sent in a decorative format with QR or serial code
The User takes the gift to the targeted store (if strict) or choose to redeem something else from same store or another The User hands the code or card to the merchant.
The merchant scans it, and gets confirmation that the code has enough credit.
The customer has to input his password to confirm the payment.
Once payment is confirmed, the merchant is credited via the mode of e-credit they’ve configured.

To the merchants.

For a User to be a merchant, they must have products, and link their mode of e-payment.
They will get the capability to scan the gift-cards of a customer.
When the card is verified, they’ll be credited on the e-payment account that they have linked.
